Is this you?

You’ve had the massages. Maybe the physio, the stretches, the ergonomic chair. The pain or stiffness keeps coming back, somehow the pattern that produces it has never been touched.

  • Chronic back pain that survives every quick fix
  • Neck, shoulder or jaw tension that returns the day after every massage
  • Posture you’ve given up trying to “hold” correctly
  • A sense that your body changed after a difficult chapter of life, and hasn’t found its way back

If any of these made you pause: that’s the work I do.

What happens here

Rolfing® Structural Integration is slow, precise, hands-on work with the body’s connective tissue and the way you organise yourself in gravity. But technique is only half of it.

My real job is to help you notice the brace you’ve been holding for years, the breath you’ve been rationing, the ground you’ve stopped trusting. Once you can feel a pattern, it stops being your fate and becomes your choice.

I don’t fix you. I help you see what’s already trying to change.

About Luke

I’m Luke Gregorczyk, one of a handful of certified Rolfing® Structural Integration practitioners in the UK, a member of the European Rolfing Association, and a bodyworker for over a decade. I came to this work because my own body once abandoned me at a moment that mattered, and I refused to accept that as the end of the story.

A few years ago my own life broke to pieces and remade itself; the practice you’ll meet today was rebuilt from that ground.

The name Aletheia is an old Greek word for the moment something hidden comes to light. It happens in bodies too. That’s the whole practice, in one word, the rest is just hands on honest bodywork.
